Arabic Dialect

There are over approximately six well-known Arabic dialects including the North African dialect, Egyptian dialect, Levantine dialect, Irawi dialect, Gulf dialect and the Yemeni dialect. Dialects use a simpler grammartical structure and include different word and pronunciation variants. This website focuses on only three of the aformentioned dialects: North African, Levantine and Egyptian.
