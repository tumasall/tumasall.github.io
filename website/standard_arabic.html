Standard Arabic

There are many different dialects in the Arabic language, but they all stem from Standard Arabic. Standard Arabic is the written language and the language of the Quran and the news. Mastering standard Arabic is difficult but it will help you to easily understand and learn all of the dialects throughout the Middle East.
